Fluffy Pancakes

Yields:
8 serving(s)

Total Time:
20 mins

Ingredients

1 c. whole milk

1 large egg

1 tbsp. vegetable oil

1 c. Homemade Pancake Mix

1 tbsp. sugar

Instructions

Step 1
In a medium bowl or measuring
cup, whisk together milk, egg,
and oil until smooth. In a large
bowl, whisk together pancake
mix and sugar; make a well in
the center and gradually whisk
in milk mixture until mostly
smooth.
Step 2
Heat a large nonstick skillet or
griddle over medium heat until
a drop of water sizzles. In
batches, add scant 1/4-cupfuls of
batter. Cook until the surface is
bubbly and edges are set,
2 to 3 minutes. With a wide spatula,
gently flip; cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
Serve rightaway or keep warm on a
baking sheet in a 225°F oven.

Step 3
Heat a large nonstick skillet
or griddle over medium heat
until a drop of water sizzles.
In batches, add scant 1/4-cupfuls
of batter. Cook until the surface
is bubbly and edges are set,
2 to 3 minutes.

Step 4
With a wide spatula, gently flip;
cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
Step 5
Serve right away or keep warm
on a baking sheet in a 225°F oven.

ALMOND-BUCKWHEAT GRANOLA
WITH YOGURT AND BERRIES

Yields:
1 c.

Total Time:
30 mins

Cal/Serv:
364

Ingredients

FOR GRANOLA

1 tbsp. olive oil

1 tbsp. pure maple syrup

1/4 tsp. kosher salt

1/2 c. buckwheat groats

1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

1/3 c. sliced almonds

FOR SERVING (1BOWL)

3/4 c. plain Greek yogurt

1/2 c. mixed berries

1/3 c. almond-buckwheat granola

Pure maple syrup, for drizzling

Instructions

Step 1
Heat oven to 300°F. Line rimmed
baking sheet with parchment paper.
In medium bowl, whisk together oil,
maple syrup and salt.
Step 2
Heat medium cast-iron skillet on
medium-high.Add groats and toast,
shaking and tossing
often and adjusting heat as needed,
until crisp and light golden brown,
2 to 4 minutes. Transfer to bowl with
maple syrup mixture and toss to coat
(it will sizzle), then stir in cinnamon
and almonds. Spread mixture onto
prepared baking sheet and bake,
stirring halfway through, until
golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
Let cool.

Step 3
Serve granola with yogurt, berries
and drizzle of maple syrup if desired.

SHEEPHERDER’S BREAKFAST

Yields:
8 serving(s)

Total Time:
30 mins

Ingredients

3/4 pound bacon strips, finely chopped

1 medium onion, chopped

8 large eggs

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon pepper

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

Step 1
In a large skillet, cook bacon
and onion over medium heat until
bacon is crisp. Drain, reserving
1/4 cup drippings in pan.
Step 2
Stir in hash browns. Cook, uncovered,
over medium heat until bottom is
golden brown, about 10 minutes.
Turn potatoes. With the back
of a spoon, make 8 evenly spaced
wells in potato mixture. Break 1
egg into each well. Sprinkle
with salt and pepper.

Step 3
Cook, covered, on low until eggs
are set and potatoes are tender,
about 10 minutes. Sprinkle with
cheddar cheese; let stand until
cheese is melted.
